Other Services

Tego IP Attorneys strives to offer its clients the best service possible at all times. We provide excellent service within the IPR sector were we aim to maximaize the value of every IPR by keeping it safeguarded at all times.

Other solutions and services that we provide to our clients are IP due diligence, IP strategy development, Corporate ID, branding and marketing, which are all disciplines and issues that are a part of or closely interweaved with intellectual property rights.

  • IP Due Diligence ↓

    Are you in need of a precise estimation of intellectual property rights? Due Diligence is a precise analysis undertaken to establish the financial, commercial, and legal aspect of a firm's assets.

    The intellectual property part of the due diligence analysis typically deals with major issues like:

    • Can the firm operate freely within the business area in question?
    • Does the firm have the relevant IP within the entire or part of the business area?
    • Are the registrations and/or pending applications as strong as claimed by the proprietor?
    • Are the IPR's valid, i.e. can the registrations and/or applications be upheld and enforced?

    For further information, please contact us at Tego IP Attorneys. We are here to service you.

  • Corporate ID ↓

    Corporate identity is the reality of your corporation. It is the unique, individual personality of the company that differentiates it from other companies and makes it special.

    When creating a new corporate identity it is crucial that the corporate identity is strong, special and reflects your brand as well as possible. It has to be trustworthy and reflect the core of your business. You also need to make sure that your corporate identity does not infringe the intellectual property rights of a third party.

    Tego IP Attorneys has established great partnership with corporate ID specialists and naming agencies and accumulated great experience in creation of corporate ID.

  • Internet ↓

    Tego IP Attorneys provides general advice and support services relating to trademark use and misuse on the Internet.

    This includes guidance on possible actions in the event of others using your trademark or infringing your rights, whether in a domain name, within a website in relation to goods or services, or when used in an inappropriate manner.

    If needed we are also able to advise on action that may be taken where domain name dispute resolution procedures are not available.

  • Branding & Marketing ↓

    "A brand is a kind of promise"

    A brand is a company's face to the world. It is expressed visually through a logo and extended through communications. A brand is how customers perceive you, including the associations and inherent value they place on your business.

    A brand is a set of fundamental principles and an organization's reason for being. It is communicated to customers, shareholders, employees and analysts. A well-planned and executed marketing strategy is needed to gain audience attention and understanding.

    Tego IP Attorneys understand and value the correlation between IPR, branding and marketing and offer our clients support and consultancy in the field of development, strategic planning, implementation and execution of their IPR, branding and marketing matters.
