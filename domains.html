Domains

In today´s society domains and the Internet are an integral part of every business. It has become essential to secure a domain before anything else is done, due to the first-come, first-served principal that is the general rule when registering a domain. The (.is) top level domain in Iceland is no exception in that regard and needs to be addressed and registered with the same principals in mind.

We recognize that it is essential to view domains as an integral part of every IP portfolio and strategy. We therefor emphasize that our clients have a solid domain strategy in place, which is aligned with the overall IP strategy of the firm before they start registering domains in Iceland as well as worldwide. Our experts have a long standing experience in developing domain strategies, assisting clients with securing the requested domains, and maintaining their domain portfolio.

  • Strategy ↓

    It is equally essential to develop a strategy for domain registrations and maintenance of domains as with other IP rights like trademarks, designs and patents.

    There are endless combinations and endings that can be registered as domains and impossible for most companies to register and own every possible ending. Tego IP Attorneys works with clients to identify their actual need for domain registrations, and to create a domain strategy that is in line with their overall IP strategy.

  • Searches ↓

    Before registering a domain name, executing a domain search is important to determine whether the domain name you have chosen is available or not, both domestically and worldwide. It may also be vital to search if some of your competitors own similar domains that might weaken your brand identity and confuse your customers in the long run.

    Tego IP Consulting offers a comprehensive search service were the spectrum is tailord to each clients specific need. Whether you need a simple, identical search or more advanced search option, our experts have both the experience and the right tools to assist you.

  • Registration ↓

    Your domain name is your online identity and securing and registering a domain name provides you with a competitive advantage.

    Domain registration can be a jungle due to the "first come, first serve" principal and the rules and requirements vary from country to country and from registry to registry.

    We have registered domains for our clients in Iceland and worldwide, ensuring and protecting their online identity and IPR's. Our professionals are knowledgeable in domain registration rules and regulations.

    Contact us for assistance with your domain matters.

  • Maintenance & Renewal ↓

    Maintaining and renewing domains is an important part of a firm's IPR work. If you fail to renew your domain names, you will lose them permanently regardless of how much you depend on them.

    Tego IP Attorneys offers an effective and efficient service for domain maintenance and renewal. By using our service, you will not need to worry about missing expiration dates or keeping track of vital login and account information for renewal.

    We act as the contact personnel with registrars, ensure all relevant data is kept safe and secured in our filing systems, and proactively contact our clients well before renewal dates to re-evaluate domain strategy and plan necessary actions. We work with trustworthy domain registrars to find the most efficient way to register our clients' domains.
