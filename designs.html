Designs

When registering a design you are protecting the appearance of your product in whole, or parts of it. The scope of protection depends on what is new and how these features are displayed in your registration.

At Tego IP Attorneys we view the importance of design registrations equal to other IP rights, and recognize the importance on developing a design registrations and protection strategy. We work with our clients on identifying valuable and registrable designs, provide guidelines and assistance with the design registration and the maintenance and renewal of the design registrations worldwide.

  • Strategy development & evaluation ↓

    The aim of any IP strategy is to create the best possible protection of your intellectual property rights and align tactically with the overall business strategy. As with other intellectual property rights such as trademarks, patents, and copyrights, design registrations are a valuable asset and their protection should be a valuable part of the overall IP strategy.

    Evaluation of the design itself and the possible scope of protection are of vital importance when registering a design both domestically and worldwide. Our experts work closely with company's on analyzing their designs and create a suitable protections strategy for their current and future products.

  • Searches ↓

    Before registrating a design it can be vital to gather information and data through design searches.

    To assist our clients with this time consuming and at times complex process, we execute design searches and analysis reports that provide information and anwers to questions that need to be asked and answered, before taking the next steps in the design registration process.

    If you need assistance with your design registrations or searches, please contact our experts for further information.

  • Registration ↓

    A registered design can be a valuable commercial asset to every firm. Design registration protects the appearance of the product or part of it, but what is registrable will depend on what is new and what is not. In many cases this can be a very complex process where professional assistance is needed.

    Our IPR professionals have extensive experience with design registration. They can safely guide you through the process of determining what can be protected and then take steps to safeguard and protect your rights. This includes guidance on the benefits and rights attached to registration and on possible remedial action in the event of third-party infringement.

    Tego IP Attorneys assists clients in registering their designs both domestically and worldwide.

  • Maintenance & Renewal ↓

    Despite being vital and strong commercially and legally, protected IP rights have such tight formal requirements that they can be easily lost or invalidated if not closely maintained and renewed in a timely manner. Our team of professionals assists in taking the responsibility of maintaining and renewing design registrations.

    We contact and remind our clients well in advance of the due dates for their IP rights' renewal and maintenance. As soon as our maintenance section receives instructions to renew a registration, we will immediately handle the renewal formalities and report the progress to the client.

  • Evaluation of and action against infringement ↓

    At Tego IP Attorneys we assist our clients with evaluating possible infringements and assist them with taking action against any illegal use of their designs. As with trademarks it is important to have an overall strategy in place for your design protection and to take action against infringements is of vital importance in the effort of safeguarding the value of the design.

  • License ↓

    Strategic licensing solutions to maximize the value of your designs while maintaining control over your creative assets and intellectual property rights.
