Trademarks

At Tego IP Attorneys, our team of experts always strive to achieve the optimal trademark protection for your trademark. Whether you are seeking an agent to work with you in Iceland, or consultancy and assistance regarding your overall IP matters our team is able to assist.

Our highly qualified experts have extensive experience in trademark protection and market analysis. By applying our method to your trademark protection strategy we contribute to maximizing the value of your trademark.

Our trademark services reaches the whole lifecycle of a trademark, from the development and creation stage of a brand, to safeguarding your investment in a fully registered brand and protect the brand from infringement or assist you with generating revenue through your brand by license and franchise agreements.

  • Strategy development & evaluation ↓

    The aim of any Intellectual Property Strategy is to create the best possible protection of your IP rights and align them with the overall business strategy. It should always be the first step before entering into registrations to get a clear picture of the goal you want to achieve by registering you trademark.

    By evaluating the strength of your brand, the target market, necessary scope of protection to achieve the set goals, countries in which to register and the time frame to reach the goals a successful strategy can be developed.

    Tego IP Attorneys offers consultancy and support in development and/or evaluation of your companies IP strategy.

  • Searches ↓

    Is your trademark valid for a registration in Iceland or internationally? Is it a strong and unique trademark that will grow in value and power, or is it a weak one that will more than likely experience fierce competition or infringements along the way?

    Tego IP Attorneys conducts trademark searches both domestically and internationally for its clients. The serivce extends to both identical trademark searches and more extensive availability searches for larger projects. To ensure the quality of the data, our experts have access to the best available databases and software, used when searching and evaluating trademarks.

    For further information about our trademark searches and our related services, please contact us at tego (at) tego.is.

  • Registration ↓

    A trademark is one of the most important business assets you'll ever own. It's your business name, slogan, logo or symbol that distinguishes your company and its products in the marketplace. For that reason it is vital to be proactive and act strategically and long-term when choosing and registering your trademark.

    Tego IP Consulting provides comprehensive service in regards to trademark registration and evaluation. Our team of experts is willing and able to assist and inform you about all aspects of registering a trademark in Iceland.

    For further information about our services and prices, please contact us through phone: +354 517 8080 or e-mail: tego (at) tego.is

  • Maintenance & Renewal ↓

    In order to prevent our clients from loosing their trademark rights due to failure of renewal, we offer safe and cost efficient service packages where our team of experts take charge of all issues concerning maintenance and renewal of your or your client trademarks.

    By using our service at Tego IP Attorneys, you can safely focus on other factors within your business without the risk of loosing one of your most valuable assets - your trademark! By letting us take charge of your trademark registration and renewals we will make sure your trademark is constantly valid and kept up-to-date. Our proffessional software and filing system will make sure of it.

    For further information or price quotes, please send an e-mail to: tego (at) tego.is and we will reply as soon as possible.

  • Watching Services ↓

    Like all other assets, trademarks must be looked after and protected, to preserve their value. Tego IP Attorneys assist our clients with watching over their trademark rights through our watching services both domestically here in Iceland and internationally.

    It is important to be well aware of new registrations that could be confused with your trademark or unauthorized use of your trademark, in order to be able to take the appropriate action. Tego offers watching services that are suitable for each business and market. Our watching services include trademark watching, domain watching and company names whether it is worldwide, regionally or nationally.

    For further information or price quote, send an e-mail to: tego (at) tego.is.

  • Opposition & Infringement ↓

    It is important to defend your trademark rights against infringements by lodging oppositions against confusing trademark registrations.

    Tego IP Attorneys assist our clients in evaluating the scope of infringement and likelihood of confusion, preparing targeted strategies and having our in-house legal team handle oppositions.

    Our team can assist you with lodging oppositions in Iceland or internationally. For further information or price quote, contact us at tego (at) tego.is.

  • Licensing & Franchise Strategies ↓

    A well established trademark that is properly registered and protected, can be more than a brand used to identify company's products or services; it can also be a source of revenue in itself. Through license and franchise agreements companies are benefitting from the full value of their trademark registrations.

    Our team of experts is experienced in advising company's in regards to licensing and franchise strategies, preparing agreements and if needed supervising the agreements on behalf of our clients.
